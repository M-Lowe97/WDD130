Michael Lowe

At 27 years old, Michael has had the opprotunity to enjoy a wide variety of games with a particular fondness for the RPG genre and its many variants. Challenge runs were initially a way to squeeze a few more hours out of expensive games, but he has since found that the sense of accomplishment that comes from overcoming a particularly difficult challenge to be rewarding.

Andrew Lowe

Andrew is a 25 year old college student who has been playing games for as long as he could remember. He chooses his challenge runs to test his knowledge and skill of the game. Overcoming the challenges gives him a unique satisfaction that only comes from difficult situations. He has a preference for turn based RPGs, Survival crafting, and simulation games.
